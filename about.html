About Us

Daacad Coffee was created to give people a peaceful place where they can enjoy quality coffee and good company. Our coffee shop is simple, clean, and welcoming.

We believe coffee is more than a drink. It brings people together, helps students focus, and gives workers energy during the day.

Our Mission

To serve fresh coffee and create a comfortable place for our customers.

Our Vision

To become one of the most loved coffee shops in our community.
